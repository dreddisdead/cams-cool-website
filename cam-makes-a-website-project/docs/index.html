About me

Hey there girly, I'm a self-taught programmer from Houston, Texas, and I somehow ended up in Cleveland, Ohio. I learned how to do everything you see on this page on my own. Please feel free to visit this page if, when reading this, youâ€™ve ever felt disheartened about programming, art, music, or any other creative expression being blocked. Coding, drawing, and creating music are the things that speak to my spirit the most. I have an insatiable curiosity for how computers and software work, ever since I received my first custom PC at the age of fourteen. Despite having no idea what I was doing, I had the most fun of my life creating little games with Unity. That's when I realized I couldn't go through life without having a computer.

I studied computer science at Penn State University but was unable to complete my degree because my mother was diagnosed with breast cancer (she is now cancer-free and living her best life). I refused to allow a piece of paper to determine whether or not I was able to do what I loved, so I returned to my roots and taught myself. Free internet resources, YouTube videos, and paid course certificates are used. Years later, I'm creating this website to display my work and support others on their path. You're welcome here regardless of how worthy you think you are! Keep checking back for updated stuff on this website. You could find something interesting, or perhaps some inspiration ;)
